ABOUT US

aQuotee is a platform built for users to find new quotes. With a simple "quote" button, users will be given a quote, and don't have to go through the hassle of finding a quote they like.

aQuotee, is built thinking about users who spend time looking for the perfect quote for their posts, tweets, and motivation. Here aQuotee will always be given to you.

To use aQuotee, simple click the "quote" button, it will give you a quote in the space below. If you want another quote, simply press it again. There are thousands of quotes waiting to be read.

If you are not satisfied with the quotes, click "Clear Quote", and it will reset itself presenting you with possibly previously-unseen quotes.
